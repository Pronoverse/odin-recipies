Butter Chicken

This fragrant, spicy Indian butter chicken recipe with yogurt marinade is from the owner of India Cuisine restaurant in Seattle, Washington.

Serve with hot Indian bread.

Ingredients

marinade:

  • ¾ pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cubed
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice, divided
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder, divided
  • salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons ginger paste
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ teaspoons garam masala

Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon chopped green chile pepper
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • ½ teaspoon dried fenugreek leaves
  • 1 cup heavy cream
